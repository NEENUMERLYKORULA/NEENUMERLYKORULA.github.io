    The places to visit in Kollam are largely of the religious genre. For instance, Sri Krishnaswamy Temple, Anandavalleswaram Temple, and Sri Mahaganapati Temple are among the most famous tourist places in Kollam. A couple of other famous religious attractions are Edavanadu Bhagwathi Temple and Holy Cross Church. However, by no means is Kollam restricted to holy places alone. You may check out fabulous locations such as Ashtamudi Lake, Thirumullavaram Beach, and Palaruvi Waterfalls among Kollam places to visit.
